# Olea Tax Co. Website Content Workbook

Use this to gather content before building in SquareSpace.

## 1. Business Basics

- Domain (already owned): OleaTaxCo.com
- Business name (exact display name):
- Tagline (short phrase under logo):
- Email:
- Phone:
- City / service area:
- Remote services offered? (yes/no):

## 2. Ideal Clients

- Who does Kelly most want to serve first?
- Individuals, families, or businesses?
- If businesses: what size/stage (solo, startup, established local business)?
- What problems do they usually have when they contact a CPA?

## 3. Services (prioritize top 3)

For each service:

- Service name
- Who it is for
- What is included
- What outcome the client gets
- How often (one-time / periodic / quarterly / annual)

## 4. Brand Voice

Pick the tone that fits Kelly best:

- Formal / expert
- Modern / direct
- Friendly / reassuring

Write 3 words that describe how clients should feel after talking to her:

- Example: clear, supported, confident

## 5. About Kelly

- Short bio (3-5 sentences)
- CPA credentials / education / experience
- Why she started her own business
- What makes her different
- Optional personal/community details

## 6. FAQs (start with 5)

Examples:

- Do you work remotely?
- What documents do I need?
- Do you help with bookkeeping cleanup?
- Do you work with new businesses?
- How do I get started?

## 7. Call to Action (pick one primary)

- Book a consultation
- Call now
- Send a message
- Request a quote

Secondary CTA (optional):

- Download checklist
- Email Kelly
- Learn more about services

## 8. Trust Builders (important for a new firm)

- CPA license / certifications
- Years of experience
- Industries served
- Client process clarity
- Response-time promise (if she wants one)
- Professional memberships

## 9. Launch Checklist (SquareSpace)

- Domain connected
- Home page
- Services page
- About page
- Contact page/form
- Privacy policy
- Mobile review complete
- Test form submission
- Proofread phone/email links
