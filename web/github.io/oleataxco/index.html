Who I am

I believe tax strategy should be intentional, not reactive.

I'm a licensed CPA with nearly a decade of experience advising entrepreneurs, multi-entity businesses, and growing founders. I've worked inside established firms where I learned both what excellent advisory looks like and where traditional processes can fall short.

Over time, I saw how messy workflows, reactive deadlines, and fragmented communication often distracted from what clients actually needed: clarity, structure, and thoughtful strategy.

I founded Olea Tax Co to work differently. Here, I work directly with each client to reduce tax exposure, improve entity structure, and ensure business decisions are made intentionally and not rushed at year-end. My approach is proactive, structured, and built around long-term thinking.
